Main Objective

The primary goal of this project is to curate a high-quality dataset that identifies harmful stereotypes related to African cultures, people, and geography in text and media. By annotating this data, you are directly contributing to training AI models to be more culturally aware, unbiased, and respectful. We aim to reduce the prevalence of "poverty porn," generalizations, and historical inaccuracies in generated content.
